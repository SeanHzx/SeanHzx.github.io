About Me

I am currently a first-year Ph.D student in Georgia Tech. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in June 2017.

My research interests are in the area of security and privacy, with special emphasis on key extraction and security problems about voice recognition. I proposed a rotation-angle-based algorithm and a roulette algorithm to use geomagnetic field for key extraction. Now I am particularly interested in addressing potential treats in voice recognition systems.
