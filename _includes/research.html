Research Experience

Secret Key Extraction from Indoor Geomagnetic Field

SJTU
2015 to 2016
  • Analyzed the space-varying and time-varying characteristics of indoor geomagnetic field with extensive measurements and observations (Roughly 2000 samples collected by smartphones in a laboratory).
  • Proposed a rotation-angle-based quantification algorithm targeting the time stamp, corresponding to the largest rotational angle of smartphones. This algorithm uses the 3-Dimension information of the magnetic field and has a high bit rate (approximately 200 bit/s).
  • Built MAGIK, a system that can extract the identical secure key between two smartphones under the presence of the malicious eavesdropper, and achieved a high authentication accuracy (91.2%).
  • Implemented the system on Nexus 7. Experiment results confirmed that our system was efficient in key extraction rate and robust in key establishment without additional overhead on mobile devices.
  • Summarized the work into a paper which was accepted in INFOCOM'17.
